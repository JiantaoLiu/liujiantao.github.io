Deep segmentation through convolutional feature learned by extra regularizer

A new kind of loss function, positive-sharing loss is proposed. This loss function comes with an additional regularizer which take more account of the wrong estimation. (Wrong label, but nonzero ones). I use this technique in some of my works. Figure.1 is a footage snapshot record 168m away from the target which is a street light located in Xiamen No. 226 LianQian West Rd. The column edge is nicely depicted by the contour. Figure.4 is a photo taken from my balcony, under a complex condition with several rich texture timbers. And old houses in Xiamen ShaPoWei in the background where other common methods fail to figure them out. CNN architecture: Input layer->COV1->COV2->COV3->COV4->FC1(Fed into a structured forest classifier)->FC2. Modified from Caffe.

Figure.1 Footage record 168m from target(Xiamen No. 226 LianQian West Rd street light) Figure.2 Comparing deep segmentation results with Canny Prewitt Sobel and Roberts

Figure.3 Randon selection of deep feature maps.

Figure.4 From balcony of home in a sunny day

Figure.5 Comparing deep segmentation results with Canny Prewitt Sobel and Roberts

Figure.6 Randon selection of deep feature maps.

Phase-based motion magnification

Phase variation is amplified to reveal the imperceptible motions. Begin with decomposing the video by steerable pyramids to separate the amplitude of the local wavelets from their phase. And then amplify or attenuate specify frequency band. Reconstruct the video in the end. This will result in less noise comparing with similar works. I was hitting the upper board of the shelf in Figure.1 .The Xiamen WuYuan Bridge cables is vibrating at 3-20hz in Figure.2.

Figures

Figure.1 Speaker on desk shelf before and after phase based magnification Figure.2 Cables of Xiamen Wu Yuan Bridge before and after phase based magnification

Reference

Wadhwa, N., et al. (2013). “Phase-based video motion processing.” ACM Trans. Graph. 32(4): 1-10.Wadhwa, N., et al. (2013). “Phase-based video motion processing.” ACM Trans. Graph. 32(4): 1-10.

Xiamen Bridges classifier based on deep learing

Using a mini data set containing 76 training images of three different bridges in Xiamen and 32 validation images. Train a network to classify confusing bridge photos. Network architecture:five convolutional layers and three fully connected layers Achive an accuracy = 93.94% *HCDQ=海沧大桥;WYDQ=五缘大桥;YWDQ=演武大桥

Image based sound frequency detection

Using a single smartphone to obtain sound frequency from desktop speaker. Proposed an image processing algorithm combined with phase-based motion magnification to obtain sound frequency from a single smartphone footage. The result from the experiments indicate the algorithm shows real promise to measure sounds.

Figure 1. Experiment setup (iPhone 7 plus on tripod, speaker working on a known input) Figure 2. Screen shot from smartphone camera Figure 3. Crop highlighted region to reduce computation Figure 4. The overall result with 80hz and 120hz sound input.
